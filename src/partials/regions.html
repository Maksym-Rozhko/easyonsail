Yacht charter in Indian Ocean

Speaking about winter yachting destinations, it is surely the Indian Ocean; the one washes shoes of Madagascar, Maldives, Seychelles and other islands. Seychelles is probably one of the best spots for skilled sailors, but charter companies may ask you to fill out a skipper summary. These islands can boast of the largest fleet in the Indian Ocean: renting a catamaran is extremely popular here since one has less sediment and can easily cruise to shallow lagoons.
